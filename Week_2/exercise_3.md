## Python arithmetic operator precedence (Order of operations)

Precedence of basic arithmetic operators `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `**`, `%`, `//`, and `()`  

#### Play basic arithmetic operator precedence

    >>> 1 + 2 - 3
    >>> 1 * 2 * 3 / 3
    >>> 10 + 10 / 3
    >>> 10 + 10 // 3
    >>> 10 + 10 % 3
    >>> 2 ** 3 + 1

#### Play with parentheses

    >>> 1 / (2 + 3)
    >>> (10 + 10) / 3
    >>> (10 + 10) // 3
    >>> (10 + 10) % 3
